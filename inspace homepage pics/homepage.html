About Inspace Furniture Solutions

“INSPACE” , The 30 years presence in the industry and the unrelenting R & D process have equipped us to plant our foot very firmly on hospital furniture making domain, now. We have a production facility which has sprung up in 1,50,000 sq.ft. Whether imported or indigenous, we use technologically advanced equipments and machineries that stimulate the development of modernization and updates it from time-to-time.

We manufacture and market hospital cots ( Simple/Fowler/Semi-fowler/ICU), bedside lockers, food trolley, structure trolley, Wheel chair, patient couch, saline stand, etc. We can handle the furniture requirement of medical centres of any size, right from a clinic to a multi-speciality hospital. Standardization of design,size & colour ensures reasonableness in pricing. Knock down construction facilitates minimal logistics cost.

Customization of furniture is also entertained in case of large volumes. We are staunch believers of saying that the business of a business is to create customers and keep them in our fold.
